About Me


Eric Simmons

Web Developer

Born and raised in the great state of Utah, I have always had my hand in several different activities and experiences. Growing up thinking I was going to be a game show host my whole entire life, I realized that I enjoy interacting with people so much that I would be a perfect fit in the communication career force. Upon receiving my Bachelors in Public Relations from Utah Valley University in 2016, I quickly found that the jobs I was applying for didn't catch my interest and I wasn't as passionate about my career choice as I had been during my time in school. Always having an interest in technology, a good friend of mine recommend that I try my hand at programming and attend DevMountain in Provo Utah. I loved every moment from beginning to end and can now proudly say I have found what I want to do for the rest of my life.



When I am not coding, you can find me geo-caching around the Salt Lake valley, rooting for Real Salt Lake or spending time with my family.

Click Event

An interactive event app

A front to back app event management app for a good user experience. Members of an event are given information of the event including Google map directions (using the maps api), taking notes during their favorite moment of the event that are saved to their account which can be accessed by signing up via Facebook or a Google+ account.

Created in 2 weeks using the following technologies: Angular, HTML, CSS, Materialize, NodeJS and SQL.

  • Date: January 2017
